Privacy Policy — KidsSong Studio

Last updated: July 7, 2026

KidsSong Studio ("the app") is a private, single-user desktop application operated by its developer and sole user ("the operator"). This policy describes how the app handles data, including data received from Google APIs.

1. Data the app accesses

  • Google OAuth tokens granted by the operator to access the operator's own YouTube channel (scopes: youtube.upload, youtube.readonly).
  • YouTube channel and video data of the operator's own channel: video IDs, titles, publishing status, and view statistics.

2. How data is used

  • Tokens are used solely to upload videos created by the operator to the operator's own channel, and to read the channel's video statistics for display in the app's local dashboard.
  • No data is used for advertising, profiling, or machine-learning model training.

3. Storage and sharing

  • All data, including OAuth tokens, is stored locally in a database file on the operator's own computer.
  • No data is transmitted to, sold to, or shared with any third party. The app has no server component accessible to anyone other than the operator.

4. Limited Use disclosure

KidsSong Studio's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Retention and deletion

Tokens and cached YouTube data are kept until the operator disconnects the channel inside the app, which deletes the stored tokens. Access can also be revoked at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
