KidsSong Studio

KidsSong Studio is a private desktop application used by a single person (the developer and owner) to manage the production and publishing workflow of children's music videos for the owner's own YouTube channel.

What the app does

  • Organizes the owner's video projects (scripts, artwork, music, rendered videos) on the owner's computer.
  • After the owner reviews and approves a finished video, uploads it to the owner's own YouTube channel using the YouTube Data API.
  • Displays view statistics of the owner's previously uploaded videos so the owner can decide what to make next.

Who can use it

The app is not distributed. It runs only on the owner's computer and connects only to YouTube channels owned by the same person. There are no third-party users.
