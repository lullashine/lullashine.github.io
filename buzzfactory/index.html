BuzzFactory Studio

BuzzFactory Studio is a private desktop application used by a single person (the developer and owner) to manage the production, quality review, and publishing workflow of educational and lifestyle videos for the owner's own YouTube channels.

What the app does

  • Organizes the owner's video projects (scripts, narration, artwork, rendered videos) on the owner's computer.
  • Runs each finished video through a quality checklist; the owner then reviews and approves it in a queue.
  • Uploads approved videos to the owner's own YouTube channels using the YouTube Data API, always with the synthetic-media disclosure flag set.
  • Displays view statistics of the owner's previously uploaded videos.

Who can use it

The app is not distributed. It runs only on the owner's computer and connects only to YouTube channels owned by the same person. There are no third-party users.
